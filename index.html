I am fascinated for the natural history of how plants are capable to defend themselves againts its natural enemies and also how they cope with abiotic factors. The current advancest of Mass spectometry, DNA sequencing and phenomics provide wonderful opportunities to study in detail the evolution and the chemical ecology and genomics of plant-herbivore interactions. I am interested to investigate the genome evolution of the species. In particular, to elucidate how genomic changes are related to phenotypic changes. I use greenhouse and field experiments, quantitative genetics with modern tools (HPLC-MS, genomics, bioinformatics) to address evolutionary questions, including generate new complete genomes for the species.
